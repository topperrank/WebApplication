Frequently Asked Question

What is the typical duration of the course?

The course’s typical duration is 2 Months. However, the duration may vary depending on factors like enrollment type, program requirements and extensions (if any). Please refer to the official course details for accurate information.

What are Bootcamps?

Bootcamps are upskilling programs that are short, intensive programs that teach specialized skills like coding, data science, or digital marketing through hands-on training. They're designed to quickly prepare participants/students for entry-level roles in these fields.

What are the eligibility requirements for the bootcamps?

Our programs cater to both students with technical and non-technical backgrounds, as well as individuals seeking job opportunities or newcomers aiming to enter specific job roles. We provide offerings for a wide range of participants.

What types of programs are offered?

We provide two types of programs: Self-Led programs, allowing you to learn at your own pace, and Mentor-Led programs, where experienced mentors guide you through the learning journey for a more interactive experience. You can choose the approach that best suits your learning style and preferences.

What is the process for changing to another batch?

Within the initial 5 days after the course commencement and after receiving your LMS credentials, you can switch batches with no additional cost. If the request is made later, a processing fee of INR 1000 will be applicable. Your enrollment will then be adjusted to the requested batch.

Could you provide an overview of the monthly milestones for the project?

Talk to our academic counselors for more details on projects.

Do you provide any discounts?

Indeed, a discount of 10% or Rs.1000 can be applied for those who make the full payment before the start of the course.

Does the program come with a job guarantee?

While there's no formal job guarantee, the curriculum aligned with industry standards will certainly enhance your resume and increase your chances of securing a promising job opportunity.

 

How frequently are batches scheduled within a month?

Two designated enrollment dates are available monthly, specifically on the 1st and the 15th. Yet, it's important to be aware that the course will start roughly one week after the chosen enrollment date, allowing time for essential prerequisites to be fulfilled.

 

When can I expect to receive the certificates?

A course completion certificate will be issued upon the successful completion of your 7-day minor project. Additionally, a project completion certificate will be granted upon the successful conclusion of your major project.
